AdColony 3.0 Release Announcement:
----------------------------------
We are pleased to announce the release of our [AdColony 3.0 SDK (Aurora)](https://github.com/AdColony/AdColony-iOS-SDK-3) that contains huge leaps forward in our capabilities, with benefits for both publishers and advertisers. In order to take advantage of the capabilities of Aurora, we are asking our partners to move to the Aurora platform for all new integrations beginning October 11, 2016 and will be removing access to our previous SDK versions. We will continue to keep our previous documentation available here for a period of time to assist in troubleshooting existing integrations.


Legal Requirements:
----------------------------------
By downloading the AdColony SDK, you are granted a limited, non-commercial license to use and review the SDK solely for evaluation purposes.  If you wish to integrate the SDK into any commercial applications, you must register an account with [AdColony](https://clients.adcolony.com/signup) and accept the terms and conditions on the AdColony website.

Note that U.S. based companies will need to complete the W-9 form and send it to us before publisher payments can be issued.
